The Best Flies According to Alex

My top 3 go-to flies

Chubby Chernobyl

There is nothing more fun than watching a trout slam a big foam fly.

Dave's Hopper

This is great for late Summer/early Fall fishing. It can also work well on the swing.

Wooly Bugger

This is the first fly I ever fished with and it will catch fish anywhere in the world.
